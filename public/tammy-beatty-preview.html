Scroll-Stopping Hooks

The First Line Is Everything

Instagram decides in 0.3 seconds whether to show your post to more people. The first line of your caption — the hook — is the single lever that controls that decision. Most lash artists write captions that describe what they did. We write lines that make someone stop mid-scroll.

❌ Original

"Just finished this set on my client — she loved it! Natural volume with a wispy finish. DM to book!"

✅ Upgraded

"The #1 mistake lash artists make on Instagram — and why it's costing you clients every single week."

The rule: Your first line must create a pattern interrupt, promise a specific outcome, or trigger curiosity. It should never describe what's already visible in the image.
